Turning brand messages into compelling visuals

Graphic designer based in Bandung with 7+ years of experience working with corporations, agencies, and growing startups. My work spans social media design, brand identity systems, marketing collateral, and UI for landing pages — always with a focus on clarity, consistency, and visual impact.
